FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking 6–12 months in advance; popular dates (like autumn) often require a year ahead. Weekdays or off-peak seasons can be arranged 3–6 months out.

Is there parking on site?

+

Yes — free on-site parking for about 30 cars. For larger guest counts we can arrange nearby overflow parking.

Do you offer guest accommodation?

+

Five on-site villas with 16 rooms are available for the night before or of the wedding. Whole-villa packages let you share warm moments with family and friends.

What if it rains during an outdoor wedding?

+

We prepare a rain contingency for every outdoor wedding — including indoor banquet hall transition and outdoor tent setup — so the day proceeds beautifully whatever the weather.

Can I bring my own vendors?

+

You’re welcome to bring your own photographer, florist or band. We also offer a list of trusted partner vendors — choose what works best for you.

Can we arrange a tasting?

+

After signing we’ll arrange a complimentary tasting — the chef will present the highlights of your wedding menu, and you can fine-tune to taste.
