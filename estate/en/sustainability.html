Design Approach

How We Build

Design Approach

01

Passive Design

Orientation, fenestration, deep eaves, natural ventilation and daylight — bring energy demand down with design first, technology second.

02

Renewable Energy

Rooftop solar, geothermal heat pumps, on-site storage — buildings power themselves and feed surplus back to the grid.

03

Water Cycle

Rainwater harvesting, greywater reuse, permeable paving and rain gardens — every drop used at least twice.

04

Local & Low-Carbon Materials

Wood, stone and steel sourced within 500 km — lower transport emissions, stronger local supply chains.

05

Indoor Air Quality

Non-toxic materials, fresh-air ventilation, biofiltration, CO&sub2; monitoring — indoor air cleaner than outside.

06

Living Landscapes

Native planting, green roofs, rain gardens, wildlife corridors — architecture extends the land, not replaces it.

Toward 2030

Net Zero by 2030

Our 2030 Promise

LOHERB Estate commits to whole-building net-zero operational carbon by 2030 across all owned and managed properties, and full life-cycle carbon neutrality by 2050.

From design through construction, operation and end-of-life recovery — every building must leave the planet’s ledger in the positive.
